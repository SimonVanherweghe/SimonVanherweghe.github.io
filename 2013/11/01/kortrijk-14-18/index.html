Kortrijk 14-18

Kortrijk in the first world war, day by day, 100 years ago.

01 Nov 2013

I had the idea to revive the first world war in Kortrijk via Twitter. This way, a tweet would pop up in your timeline and would make you think about it for a minute.

It turned out that Egied van Hoonacker wrote a book about the day-by-day events in Kortrijk during that period of time. This was a goldmine to say the least. I used the book as a source for the tweets.

https://twitter.com/Kortrijk1418/
